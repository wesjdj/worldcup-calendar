How does subscribing work?
  • Subscribed feeds update automatically. Knockout fixtures currently say "Winner Group A" etc.; when results come in, the events update in your calendar — no re-import needed.
  • iPhone / Apple Calendar: tap the Apple button. Safari hands the feed to the Calendar app and asks to confirm.
  • Outlook desktop: the Apple button uses webcal://, which Outlook for Windows/Mac picks up directly.
  • Google Calendar: opens the "add by URL" dialog — sign in once, then the calendar appears under "Other calendars".
  • One-off import: use the .ics download. Events won't auto-update.
